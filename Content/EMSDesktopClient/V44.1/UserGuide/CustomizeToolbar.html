Customize Your Toolbar

To customize your EMS Desktop Client toolbar, follow these steps:

  1. Right-click the toolbar and click Customize. 

    The Customize Toolbar dialog box opens.

  2. Customize your icons as follows:

    • To display the toolbar icons as large icons, select Large Icons.

    • To display the toolbar icons as small icons, select Small Icons. 

    • To add or remove icons from the toolbar display, select the icons (CTRL+click to pick more than one at a time) and then use the arrow buttons in the middle, or double-click them, to move them between the Hide or Show panels.

    • To change the order (from left to right) in which the icons are displayed on the toolbar, click Move Up or Move Down.

  3. Click OK to save your changes and update the toolbar, or click Cancel to exit without saving your changes.
