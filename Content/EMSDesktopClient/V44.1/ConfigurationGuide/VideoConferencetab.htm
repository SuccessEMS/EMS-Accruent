Video Conference Tab

The Video Conference tab is available only in EMS Enterprise and only if the Video Conference option was selected on the Everyday User Template tab. See Configure Video Conference Capabilities for information on how to configure the information on this tab correctly so that users who are attached to this everyday user process template can request rooms that have a video conferencing features.

To configure video conference settings for an everyday user process template, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Video Conference tab of the Everyday User Process Template dialog box.

Video Conference Tab

  1. From the drop-down list(s), make the appropriate selection(s).
