Configure Groups (Toolbar Icon)

Groups Are Often Re-Labeled "Employees" or "Customers"

Groups is an icon on the application toolbar. The label "Groups" is generic and is the default label delivered with EMS Desktop Client. The Groups label is often re-labeled "Employees" or "Customers." Groups can be associated with Billing and PO numbers, booking templates, and so on, and greatly affect what your end-users have access to during the booking process.
