System Administration > Settings

In addition to configuring core data items that are specific to your organization, you must configure settings that determine a variety of operational factors for your EMS installation, such as system-wide parameters, registration information, email settings, and Reservation Wizard required fields.

You can access the System Administration settings under System Administration > Settings.

The following topics provide information about the settings found in System Administration:

  • Edit System Parameters
  • Import Registration Information
  • Configure the Confirmation Email Subject Line
  • Reservation Wizard Required Fields
  • Configure Email Settings
  • Set Global Email Preference to SMTP and My Personal Email Preference to Outlook
