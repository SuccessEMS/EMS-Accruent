Configuration > Kiosk

The EMS Kiosk is a powerful yet easy-to-use digital room sign and interactive tool for reviewing room availability, booking space, searching for a meeting location and more. Mount the unit outside a room and/or make an EMS Kiosk available in a lobby or entryway.

You can access the Kiosk configuration settings under Configuration > Kiosk.
