Services and Components

This section discusses additional services and components offered by EMS Software. These services and components include:

  • EMS Automated Report Service

  • EMS Email Notification Service—This optional service notifies users via email of changes in the database.

  • EMS Check-in Notification Service—This service automates the process of reminding users to check in and managing no-shows. You configure Check-in Notification rules based on rooms, statuses, event types, and/or group types.

  • EMS Campus Auto Sync Service (EMS Campus Only)—This feature automates the process of allowing Domain Schedulers to manually process post-publish course changes made in your SIS to EMS.
