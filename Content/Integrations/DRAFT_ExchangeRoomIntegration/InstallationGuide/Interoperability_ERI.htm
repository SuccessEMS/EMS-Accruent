ERI Interoperability Between EMS Products and Exchange

This topic provides information on how Exchange Room Integration works with EMS products to sync booking data and whether there are any limitations with the data sync. The information is divided into two sections based on the sync direction:

  • Interoperability of ERI  Sync

  • Interoperability of ERI Sync

Interoperability of ERI  Sync

The ERI  Sync allows users to book rooms/resources linked to an Exchange mailbox from native Exchange applications, such as Windows Outlook, Macintosh Outlook, Outlook web, and Outlook Mobile. Exchange will push these native bookings and updates to EMS.
