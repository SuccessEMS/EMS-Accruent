New Booking

Recurrent Bookings During Daylight Savings Caused Error

When users booked recurrent meetings in EST that had occurrences during daylight savings (after March 12), those meetings showed on the calendar as one hour later than they should have been.

Fix: Modified functionality so all bookings times match and daylight savings is properly accounted for.
