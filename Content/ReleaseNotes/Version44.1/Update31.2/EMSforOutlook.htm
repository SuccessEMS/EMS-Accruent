Booking Reflected Incorrect Time in Schedule Grid During Edit for Time Zones with GMT Offset with 30 or 45 Minutes

When editing a booking in EMS for Outlook, the calendar grid did not reflect the correct time for meetings in a time zone where GMT had a 30 or 45 minute offset. The booking time and appointment time were correct upon create.

Fix: Modified functionality so that the time reflects correctly in the EMS calendar grid when editing.
