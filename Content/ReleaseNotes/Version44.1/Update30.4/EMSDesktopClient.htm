Expert User Permissions: Modify Booking Details

The Modify Booking Details expert user process was not behaving as expected, as users could not add/edit/delete the category and resources of booking detail items without additional permissions.

Fix: Modified functionality so that the Modify Booking Details security process allows expert users to add/edit/delete booking details on any booking they have permission to access.
