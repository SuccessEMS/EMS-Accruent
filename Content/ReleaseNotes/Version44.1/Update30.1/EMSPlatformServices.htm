Response Issue With Rooms/actions/availability

A change to the behavior of the rooms/actions/availability call was made to cause passing of an empty WebUserId or a WebUserId equal to 0 with the Client token to fail due to having an invalid relationship with the templates also passed in the call. This change also caused passing a WebUserId equal to 0 with a UserToken to fail due to the WebUserId having an invalid relationship with the templates passed in the call. Both of these changes have been reverted.

The following routes have been updated:

  • POST/rooms/action/availability
  • POST/rooms/action/availability/templates

Fix: Updated GA routes for single and multi-templates so they return results with webuser id -0.
