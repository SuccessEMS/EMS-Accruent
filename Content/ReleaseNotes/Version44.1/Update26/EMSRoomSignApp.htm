Time Bar

Time Bar and Check In Button Displayed Wrong Color

The check in button and time bar were not displaying as orange but remained green before and during booking time when the check in button was not yet clicked.

Fix: Modified functionality so that the check in button and time bar display as orange before and during booking time if the check in button is not yet clicked.

Internal Reference # 36241
(For EMS Internal Use Only)

ArmorActive Integration

EMS Room Sign App Running on iOS 11 Devices and ArmorActive Enclosures Not Syncing

iPads running on iOS 11 or greater were playing sounds regardless of any user interaction inactivity. Devices running on iOS 11 suspended the Audio Context after a period of inactivity, and were not properly resumed when a new sound was needed to be played.

Fix: Modified functionality so that when the Room Sign App completes polling, the LEDs are triggered.

Internal Reference # 39388
(For EMS Internal Use Only)
