Performing a Hard Refresh

Enhancements often require a hard refresh of the End User’s browser cache. This topic provides instructions on performing a hard refresh by browser:

  • Google Chrome

  • Internet Explorer

  • Safari

  • Mozilla Firefox (and related browsers)

Google Chrome

  1. Hold the Ctrl key and press the F5 key.

  2. Or, hold the Ctrl key and click the Refresh button.

Internet Explorer

  1. Hold the Ctrl key and press the F5 key.

  2. Or, hold the Ctrl key and click the Refresh button.

Safari

  1. Hold down Shift and click the Reload button on the toolbar.

Mozilla Firefox and Related Browsers

Windows/Linux

  1. Hold the Ctrl key and press the F5 key.

  2. Or, hold down Ctrl and Shift and then press R.

Mac

  1. Hold down the Shift and click the Reload button.

  2. Or, hold down Cmd and Shift and then press R.
