Home and My Reservations Pages

 

Enhancement to Make EMS Kiosk App Display More Responsive for 1080 x 862 Resolution

The following enhancements were made to the Home page and the My Reservations page to make them more responsive at a 1080 x 862 resolution: 

  • Home Page — The I don't have my badge button now displays on the page without blocking the other menu items at the bottom of the page.
  • My Reservations Page — The list of bookings now displays without requiring users to scroll to the right to view all of the booking details.

Locate Space
(Legacy EMS Kiosk App)

 

Locate Space Page Incorrectly Displayed Rooms as Reserved

For the Legacy version of the EMS Kiosk App, when users searched for available rooms from the Locate Space page, rooms with same-day reservations were showing as unavailable (red icon) even though the rooms were currently available. For example, if Room A was reserved from 10:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. and a user searched for rooms available from 8:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m., Room A would show as unavailable.

Fix: Modified functionality so that the Locate Space page accurately displays room availability.
