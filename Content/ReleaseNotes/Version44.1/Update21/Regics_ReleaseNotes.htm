Payments

Complete Payments Were Shown As Incomplete

Regics showed payments as incomplete even though the payment was completed on the CyberSource Gateway. No error logs were generated and no popups were blocked on the CyberSource Gateway.

Fix: Modified functionality so that Regics honors the completed payments from the CyberSource Gateway.
