Not all bookings showing on schedule view grid when completing a room search

Not all bookings are being displayed on the schedule grid when searching for a room. This is due to a logic error where the value in the parameter Number of Available Rooms Upon Search is being used to limit the results returned on booking searches.

For customers who are affected and require a workaround, submit an EMS Support Help Desk ticket through to receive additional information.
