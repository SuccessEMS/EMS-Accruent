2-Way Sync

2-Way Sync – Subscribe to room mailboxes for Exchange bookings and sync single and recurring bookings

The new 2-Way Sync feature allows EMS to retrieve bookings from Exchange and synchronize booking information. You can subscribe to Exchange room mailboxes and receive push notifications from Exchange for the following changes to single and recurring bookings: 

  • New bookings in Exchange resource calendars

  • Modifications to existing bookings in Exchange resource calendars

  • Deletions of bookings in Exchange resource calendars

2-Way Sync > EMS Kiosk App

2-Way Sync with Kiosk 2.0 – Ability to sync single and recurring Exchange Integration bookings

The EMS Kiosk App can now sync with Exchange Integration Web Service (EIWS). This sync allows single and recurring Exchange bookings made in the Kiosk App to appear on room calendars and on organizer/attendee Outlook calendars so that room and attendee availability are accurate.

For the 2-Way Sync to work between the EMS Kiosk App and EIWS, a 1:1 mapping must exist between the group and the Everyday User.

The POST/Kiosk/{id}/reservation route was modified for this enhancement.

EMS Internal Reference # EMS-5309 and EMS-5310
(For Internal Use Only)

2-Way Sync > EMS Kiosk App

2-Way Sync with Kiosk 2.0 – Ability to cancel single and recurring Exchange Integration bookings

With 2-Way Sync, users can now cancel single and recurring Exchange bookings from the EMS Kiosk App, and the canceled bookings will be removed from room calendars and from organizer/attendee Exchange calendars so that room and attendee availability are accurate.

The POST/Kiosk/{Id}/booking/group/{groupId}/cancel route was modified for this enhancement.

EMS Internal Reference # EMS-5499 and EMS-5500
(For Internal Use Only)

2-Way Sync > EMS Kiosk App

2-Way Sync with Kiosk 2.0 – Ability to end Exchange Integration bookings

With 2-Way Sync, users can now end Exchange bookings from the EMS Kiosk App, and the bookings end time will be updated in room calendars and in organizer/attendee Exchange calendars so that room and attendee availability are accurate.

The POST/kiosk/{id}/booking/{bookingId}/group/{groupId}/endnow route was modified for this enhancement.

(For Internal Use Only)
