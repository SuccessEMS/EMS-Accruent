Time Zone

Unable to book buildings after 2019-10-27 in the Jerusalem time zone

Users were unable to book buildings after the date 2019-10-27 in the Jerusalem time zone (ID=13). When users tried to book these buildings in EMS Web App, they would get an "internal server error," and in EMS Desktop Client, they would get the error "insert fails." This issue occurred because the last EndDate for tblTimeZone was 2019-10-27.

Fix: Added rows for Jerusalem time zone (ID=13) to tblTimeZone through 2029.
