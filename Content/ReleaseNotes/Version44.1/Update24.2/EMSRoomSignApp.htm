EMS Room Sign App LEDs De-Sync Following iPad Inactivity

iPads running on iOS ≥ 11 was playing sounds regardless of any user interaction inactivity. Devices running on iOS 11 will suspend the Audio Context after a period of inactivity, and it is not properly resumed when a new sound is needed to be played.

Fix: Modified functionality so that when the Room Sign App completes polling, the LEDs are triggered.
