Area

Edit Booking

Extending Meeting Duration Caused Error

When users changed the end time for a meeting to extend meeting duration, the error message "Room no longer available" incorrectly displayed.

Fix: Modified validations to ensure that this error message no longer presents and users can successfully extend the meeting duration.

Internal Reference # 28194
(For EMS Internal Reference Only)

Timezones

Time Zones/Daylight Savings Not Synched With Device

When users worked with meetings that were booked in different time zones than the time zone for their mobile device, daylight savings time adjustments were not properly synched and booking times appeared to be off by an hour in the list view, on the reservation, and on booking details.

Fix: Modified validations to ensure that daylight savings time is properly accounted for when syncing time zones between users' mobile devices and meeting times in the database, such that times display to users in the set time zone of their mobile devices.

Internal Reference # 28196
(For EMS Internal Reference Only)
