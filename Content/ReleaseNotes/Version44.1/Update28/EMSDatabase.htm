Error Opening Room Information in EMS Desktop Client

Some users were receiving error messages (such as "Insert statement failed..." or "Delete statement failed...") when they tried to open room information in the EMS Desktop Client.

Fix: Modified functionality so that there are no errors when opening room information in the EMS Desktop Client.
