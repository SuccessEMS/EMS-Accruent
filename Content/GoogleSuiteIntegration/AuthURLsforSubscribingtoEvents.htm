Authorize URLs for Subscribing to Events

To authorize URLs for subscribing to events from GSuite:

  1. Navigate to this link.

  2. Ensure you have the correct project selected.

  3. Enter your EMS Cloud Services Domain (for example: my-subdomain.emscloudservice.com).

  4. Click Add Domain.

  5. The Verify Ownership dialog box will appear. Click Take Me There.

    Verify Ownership Dialog Box

  6. The Webmaster Central dialog box will appear. Sign in to your domain name provider.

  7. Click the Other drop-box list and click Add a CNAME record.

    Webmaster Central Dialog Box

  8. Provide the CNAME Label / Host and the CNAME Destination / Target to EMS to be applied to DNS for validation.

    Providing the CNAME Label/Host and Destination Target

  9. Once EMS has put the records in place, you can click Verify.

    If you end up away from this page, you can follow the steps again and it will produce the same records.
