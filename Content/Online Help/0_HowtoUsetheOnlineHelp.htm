How to Use the Online Help

This topic will teach you how to use the components of the Online Help.

Toolbar

The Toolbar appears in the upper-right corner of all topics in the Online Help.

Button Name Function
Home This button takes you to the home page of your product's documentation.
Print

This button opens a system print dialog.

You must expand the sections you want to include in your printed document.
LTR PDF This button opens a letter-sized PDF of the Online Help.
A4 PDF This button opens an A4-sized PDF of the Online Help.
Navigate Previous This button opens the previous page in the Table of Contents.
Navigate Next This button opens the next page in the Table of Contents.

Expand All / Collapse All This button either expands or collapses all expanding sections on the page.

Search Field

The Search field appears in the upper-right corner of the Online Help. The Search field will return a list of topics that contain your search term in their titles and topics that contain your search term in the body of the topic.

To perform a search of the Online Help, follow the steps below:

  1. Enter your search term in the Search field.

    A list of topics that match your search term appear in the drop-down menu.

  2. Choose between two options:

    • Select the first option in the drop-down menu.

      Clicking the first option in the Search drop-down menu will take you to a list of Search results that match your Search criteria. You can then preview the content of the pages in the Online Help, and select which page sounds relevant to your needs.

      Search option.

      Search results page.

    • Select the one of the topics that appears in the drop-down menu.

      Selecting a topic from the drop-down menu opens the topic in the Online Help.

      Topic option.
