Online Help

Online Help provides easy access to relevant system information. The Online Help system is context-sensitive—when you click the Help link on any page, information specific to that page is automatically displayed.

You can navigate through the online help system using elements in the navigation pane on the left side of the window or in the toolbar in the upper-right side of a topic.

  • Table of Contents (TOC) – The table of contents lists all topics within the online help system. Clicking a link displays the selected topic and expands the selected topic to show any sub-topics beneath it.

  • Search – Click Search to perform a text-based search of the entire online help system. Searches are not case-sensitive and will look for any of the terms entered. To search for a phrase, enclose it in quotation marks, for example "default contact."

  • Toolbar – Click the icons in the topic toolbar to perform various functions such as navigating to a previous topic in the Table of Contents, opening the online help home page, or expanding and collapsing the expanding sections in a topic. As your mouse hovers over an icon, a quick tip about the function it performs is displayed.
