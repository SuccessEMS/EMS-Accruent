User Options

When your site administrator sets you up as a user in a Master Calendar system, you are supplied with both your User ID and password. You can change your own password, or you can have current password emailed to you if you have forgotten it. In addition, you can view a history of all the event requests that you have submitted, and you can view a summary of all your calendar subscriptions and reminders.

This section will provide information that will allow you to:

  • Changing Your Password
  • Viewing Your Notifications
  • Viewing Your Requests
