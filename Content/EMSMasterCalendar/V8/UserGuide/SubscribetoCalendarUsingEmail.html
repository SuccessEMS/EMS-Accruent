Subscribe to a Calendar Using Email

Once you have begun the process of subscribing to a calendar, you can subscribe using email as follows. See Also: Subscribe to a Calendar.

  1. If you are a guest, enter the email that is to be used for your subscription in the Email field. Optionally, as a user, you can edit this email address if needed.
  2. Select one or both of the options that are available for email subscriptions—email notifications for new events and email notifications for changes and cancellations.
  3. Click Subscribe. A message confirms that an email has been sent and prompt you to confirm your subscription request.
  4. Click Done to close the message and return to the Calendar Display page.
